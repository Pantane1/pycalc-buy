# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## What We Collect

SciCalc Pro collects only what is necessary to process payments:

- **Phone number** — used solely to initiate M-Pesa STK Push via Lipana.dev
- **Transaction ID** — used to verify and activate your license
- **License expiry** — stored temporarily in server memory to validate access

## What We Do NOT Collect

- No names, emails, or personal profiles
- No calculation history or usage data
- No cookies or tracking scripts
- No data is sold or shared with third parties

## Payment Processing

All payments are handled by **Lipana.dev** and **M-Pesa (Safaricom)**. SciCalc Pro never sees or stores your M-Pesa PIN or payment credentials.

## Data Retention

License data is held in server memory only for the duration of your active plan (7 or 30 days). It is not written to any database or persistent storage.
